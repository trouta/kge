Culture Presentations

As of 2018-2019, K-GE E-Board has brought more of the Korean culture to K-GE by giving information presentations throughout the semester on various aspects of Korea culture, politics, and daily life. While past presentations have been given by each E-Board member, a new E-Board position may be created solely for this role.
