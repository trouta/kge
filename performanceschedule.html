Performance Schedule

K-GE is invited to perform to several events each semester, including those hosted by on-campus and off-campus organizations. Members have the option to sign up for as many performances as they want, and K-GE will perform a mix of songs for each event. In addition to outside events, K-GE also hosts its own showcase, K-Pop Unleashed (also known as K-GE Show) in the winter semester, typically on the Thursday before spring break. For K-Pop Unleashed, K-GE invites guest performers in addition to performances by K-GE that include intro, main group, small groups, and E-Board.
