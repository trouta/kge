Constitution

The K-GE constitution outlines policies related to member eligibility, attendance expectations, executive board roles and responsibilities, as well as our overall purpose. Additional notes include our affiliated organizations, mix requirements, and any other information we believe beneficial to outlining the structure and function of K-GE as an organization. We highly encourage all members to be familiar with the constitution, as it defines how the executive board runs K-GE and how K-GE functions as a college organization.
