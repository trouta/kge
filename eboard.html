Election Process

K-GE Policy

In order to run for an E-Board position, you must be nominated by a member of the general assembly or current E-Board. Nominees have the option to accept or deny their nomination; should they accept, they will be required to present a speech to the general assembly.

Speeches will be held Sunday, March 17th and Monday, March 18th. Voting will take place on March 18th following the last speech. If you are unable to attend Monday's practice but are still interested in voting, please speak to an E-Board member.

Upcoming Elections

E-Board nominations for 2019-2020 have closed. Elections for the 2020-2021 E-Board will take place after 2020 K-Pop Unleashed, and new E-Board members must be selected by March 31st, 2020.
